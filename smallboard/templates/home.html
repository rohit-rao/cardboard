The Intermittent Puzzboard

  • What is this?
    A dashboard for managing puzzles and tracking status during a puzzle hunt, such as MIT Mystery Hunt:
  • Why do I have to sign in?
    We use Google login to ensure that users only see puzzles for the hunts they are participating in.
  • How are you using my data?
    We match your email against an allowlist of emails for each hunt. The actions you take while using the smallboard (such as solving a puzzle) may be logged and attributed to your email. This data is only used for logging purposes. See here for our full privacy policy.
  • Cool. Why does it say "Your credentials aren't allowed" when I try to sign in?
    You are currently not allowlisted for any active hunts. If you believe you should have access or would like to be allowlisted for a hunt, please ask someone. If you don't know who to ask, this probably isn't the site for you.
